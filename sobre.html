Sobre MGA Entertainment

Founded in 1979, MGA Entertainment has built a reputation of creating top consumer brands across a wide variety of consumer product categories, continually providing innovative play patterns that appeal to girls & boys of all ages. MGA Entertainment, the world’s largest private toy company, has a diversified portfolio of award-winning brands such as L.O.L. Surprise!, Little Tikes, Rainbow High, MGA's Miniverse, Story Dream Machine, Fluffie Stuffiez, Bratz, Dream Ella, Foamo, Tobi Robot Smartwatch, Zapf Creation and many more of your favorite toys!.
